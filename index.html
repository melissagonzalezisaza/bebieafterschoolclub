PRACTICAL INFO
Everything You Need to Know 📋

Bebies After School Club gives children a safe, nurturing space to learn, create and grow — every weekday afternoon.

📚

Homework & Literacy Support

Children can complete their homework in our calm, supervised environment. We provide dedicated time for literacy work, reading practice and numeracy — so children arrive home with it done!

🕒

Mon–Fri, 3:30pm – 5:30pm

We run every weekday during term time. Late pick-up may be available subject to availability — please contact us to discuss.

🚌

School Collection

We collect children directly from local W9 schools, making the transition from school to club completely seamless for families.

📍

MEET OUR FOUNDER
Hello, I'm Bebie!

My name is Bebie Waller, the Programme Director of Actingworks UK. I began Actingworks a few years ago while searching for exciting after-school activities for my son — and discovered I could combine my career in theatre, TV and radio with my love of teaching.

I'm a founding member of the repertory theatre Avalon in New York, where we had our own Broadway theatre. I've written for theatre and radio, and directed and produced many shows throughout my first career.

Working with children has been a joy throughout my time as an actress, coach and mentor. My passion for theatre, film, music, dance, reading and yoga is highly infectious — and I want every child to catch it!
